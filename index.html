About VIREON

Bridging the Gap Between Innovation and Access

VIREON Healthcare Limited was founded on a single conviction: that patients in Africa deserve the same access to breakthrough medicines as anywhere else in the world. We source, import, and distribute novel and niche pharmaceutical products, navigating regulatory complexities so that healthcare providers can focus on care.

We work directly with global manufacturers and authorised distributors to source breakthrough therapies, navigating complex import processes so healthcare providers and patients don't have to.

Patient Guidance

Patient Resources

We know navigating access to novel medications can feel overwhelming. Here's what you need to know.

VIREON supplies directly to licensed healthcare providers, hospitals, specialist clinics, and registered pharmacies. If you are a patient, the best path is through your doctor or specialist who can raise an inquiry on your behalf. You can also contact us directly via the form below and we'll guide you to the appropriate provider in your area.
Affordability is central to our mission. We work with manufacturers and institutional buyers to negotiate the most competitive pricing possible for the African market. For patients on novel or rare disease therapies, we also maintain information about available Patient Access Programmes and compassionate use options where they exist.
Every product we handle is sourced exclusively from licensed manufacturers or authorised distributors. We do not deal in grey-market or parallel imports, full stop. Documentation and batch information are provided on request for every therapy we supply.
Our portfolio spans multiple therapeutic areas, including Type 2 Diabetes, Obesity, Sickle Cell Disease, HIV/AIDS, Oncology, Cardiology, and Rare Diseases. We specifically focus on novel and niche treatments that are not commonly available in the African market, giving patients access to therapies previously only available abroad.
Absolutely. While our supply chain operates through healthcare providers, we welcome direct enquiries from patients and their families. Use the contact form below or reach us via email at info@vireonhealthcare.com. We'll direct you to the right resources or the nearest participating provider.
